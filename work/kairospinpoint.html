Kairos Pinpoint

Networked Insights

Kairos® Pinpoint helps marketers get to the "why" of a trend faster.

Problem

Pinpoint was a new product functionality to be added to Kairos. The analytics capability had been prototyped during a hackathon and it was the product team's task to incorporate it into Kairos, Networked Insights' SaaS product.

Strategy

After exploring the capabilities in the hackathon prototype, I worked alongside the Product Owner, Product Manager, and Business Analyst to plan and conduct user research interviews around the existing search capabilities and analysis process. That insight was incoporated into everything that followed.
